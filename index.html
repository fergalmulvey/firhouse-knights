Why you should sign up?

Play and Learn in a Relaxed, Friendly Environment

Becoming a "Knight" offers a myriad of enriching experiences and opportunities. Whether you're a seasoned player seeking to hone your skills or a newcomer eager to learn, our club provides tailored lessons that cater to all levels of expertise. Beyond the board, we foster a vibrant social community through regular meetups and outings, where friendships flourish amidst shared passions for the game. In our relaxed and welcoming environment, members have the chance to engage in friendly competition during tournaments, further refining their strategies while enjoying the camaraderie of fellow chess enthusiasts. Joining us isn't just about playing chess—we're about embarking on a journey of growth, learning, and connection in a supportive and inclusive setting.

Adult membership is priced at €90, while Students and u18s can avail of a €30 discount.
